About Us

The Eliott Hotel,
Gibraltar

The Eliott Hotel is located in the very heart of Gibraltar on 2 Governors’ Lane, right beside the Town Main Street and just 2km from Gibraltar Airport. All of Gibraltar’s top landmarks and attractions are within a short walking distance, including Gibraltar Marina, the Gibraltar Cable Car, Casement’s Square and the Botanical Gardens.

Boasting the ultimate outdoor amenities and best central location in the Old Town, the Eliott is the perfect hotel for any stay in Gibraltar.

What We Do

Discover Our Services

The Gibraltar Pass

The Gibraltar Pass is your key to accessing Gibraltar’s top attractions. The pass covers discounted entry to the Rock’s most visited attractions and landmarks as well as special offers and freebies at some of Gibraltar’s top restaurants and bars, including our rooftop restaurant, the Rock Salt!

Restaurant Service

The Rock Salt is the rooftop restaurant, located on the 8th floor of The Eliott Hotel. Featuring magnificent, panoramic views of Gibraltar, it is an idyllic spot for guests to sit back and take in the stunning views of the Mediterranean coastline. Food is served from 7am to 11pm everyday.

Bedrooms & Suites

Our Rooms are decorated in a unique, Mediterranean style, featuring colorful artwork and beautiful furnishings, taking inspiration from the Mediterranean coastline. Featuring premium beds with air conditioning, walk-in showers and a large flat screen TVs.

Amenities

Soak up the Mediterranean sun at our roof top pool, featuring spectacular views of the Gibraltar Straits. A spacious lounge and seating area surrounds the pool, offering a peaceful oasis, the perfect spot for an evening cocktail with friends or family. Guests can order food or drink by the pool from morning into evening.

Customer Service

Save up to 25% off our Best Available Rate when staying 5 nights or more! Enjoy our comfy rooms with breakfast every morning of your stay, the best outdoor amenities and dining offerings while unwinding right in the heart of Old Town Gibraltar, right next to the Main Street and within walking distance of the Rock’s top landmarks.

Bar & Drink

The Veranda Bar and Terrace is situated on the ground floor, right on the doorstep of Gibraltar’s lively Old Town. The bar is a modern space to unwind or mingle with a drink with indoor or outdoor seating options for guests. Offering an extensive drinks menu, it is the ideal spot for guests to unwind after a long day exploring.
